Magic Bus & Laureus Charity Auction for Education

Magic Bus and Laureus Sport for Good are hosting a charity auction to raise funds for helping 1 million children get back to school in India. Several individuals from sport and entertainment have donated prized memorabilia and exclusive experiences to provide you with money-can’t-buy items. All proceeds from the auction will go directly towards the Childhood to Livelihood Programs in schools & communities across India.
